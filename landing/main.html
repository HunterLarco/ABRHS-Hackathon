WHAT IS HackAB?

HackAB is Acton-Boxborough's two day hackathon. Students will learn how to program and use their talents to build, create, and innovate. Our focus is to give students a window into the ever-growing field of technology at any technical level, making it an excellent learning opportunity for both the new and experienced.

FAQ

What is a Learnathon?

A set of interactive classes and fun workshops designed to teach something new outside of a normal curriculum regarding programming and technology.

What if I've never coded before?

If you have never coded before, don’t worry! Students will teach and mentor you along the way. At the start of the event, there will be sessions to help you install the right development tools and environments to start coding.

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where people collaborate to create cool apps and software projects!

How big should teams be? Can I do it alone?

Teams should be at most 4 people. You can do it alone, but it'll be less fun! At the beginning of the event we will hold a team building conference. If you'd like to join a team or want more members we'd be happy to help then.

When is the event?

We don't know yet. We're working actively with the administration and senior class officers to put a date on the calendar. We'll make sure that everyone knows as soon as we do!
