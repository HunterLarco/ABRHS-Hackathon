What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where people collaborate to create cool apps and software projects!

Yes! In fact we encourage it. A lot of programming requires teamwork and communication skills. At the end of the event you will be able to see what your fellow students have created and we will select three teams to demonstrate their creations infront of everyone.
